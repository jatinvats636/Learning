Facts

Some fun facts about dogs

  1. Big happy " helicopter" tail wagging is one sign of a really nice dog
  2. Tired puppies get cranky just like little kids. If you have a fussy puppy, try nap time.
  3. Stroking dogs and gazing into their eyes releases the "feel good" hormone oxytocin for both people and dogs.
  4. Just like human fingerprints, no two dogs' nose prints are alike.
  5. At about 6 inches, the Chihuahua is the shortest breed.
  6. A Russian dog named Laika was the first animal in space, traveling around Earth in 1957.
  7. The best age to bring a puppy home is 8 to 12 weeks.
  8. New puppies have heat sensors in their noses to help find their moms while their eyes and ears are closed.
  9. Therapy dogs, who bring healing to individuals and families by visiting hospitals, schools or retirement homes, differ from service dogs, who assist individuals who have disabilities.
  10. Dogs sweat through the pads of their feet.
  11. Petting a dog can lower your blood pressure.
  12. Dogs can get jealous when their humans display affection toward someone or something else.
  13. Dogs can be trained to detect cancer and other diseases in humans
  14. Dogs are mentioned in the Bible more than 35 times
  15. Obesity is the top health problem among dogs.
